I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My advisor is Professor Ryan Sriver.

My research seeks to better understand the uncertainties surrounding projections of future climate and the associated risks of climate change. I am particularly interested in the challenges of generating local-scale climate information, and how we can ensure that adaptation decisions are robust to the full range of possible climate futures.
