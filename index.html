I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My advisor is Professor Ryan Sriver.

My research focuses on understanding how well we can simulate climate and weather extremes and their impacts on coupled human-environment systems such as agriculture. More broadly, I am interested in applying the frameworks of multi-sector dynamics and decision-making under uncertainty to the problem of climate change adaptation.
